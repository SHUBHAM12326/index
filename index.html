Robber's Cave

Robber's Cave locally known as Gucchu Pani, is a river cave formed in the Himalayas. This river cave is believed to be an abode of Lord Shiva. Its speciality is that river water flows out from the middle of the cave.Robber's Cave is a river cave formation that is about 600 meters long. It is divided into two main parts, with the central part featuring a waterfall. The cave is characterized by its high, narrow walls, with a stream of water flowing through it, which adds to its mystical and scenic appeal.The name "Robber's Cave" is derived from local folklore which suggests that the cave was used by robbers to hide and escape from the British authorities during the colonial period. Although this story adds an element of intrigue, there is no concrete historical evidence to support. Learn more

Sahastradhara

Sahastradhara meaning 'thousand fold spring' is popular for its ecstatic beauty and therapeutic value since its water contains Sulphur. Its picturesque beauty attracts a lot of travellers.Sahastradhara is known for its numerous water springs, which are believed to have medicinal properties due to their high sulfur content. The water flows through limestone stalactites, resulting in the deposition of sulfur over time. The area features a beautiful landscape with waterfalls, caves, and lush greenery, making it a picturesque spot for visitors.The best time to visit Sahastradhara is during the post-monsoon season, from September to March, when the weather is pleasant and the springs are at their most picturesque. The summer months, from April to June, are also a good time to visit, but the area can get crowded with tourists.Learn more

Tapkeshwar Temple

Tapkeshwar Temple is a famous cave temple on the banks of a rivulet. The water further flows in to the cave to drip on to the Shiv Linga inside. Sulphur springs present around the temple makes for a therapeutic bath. This cave was once the abode of the famous guru Dronacharya and hence is also known as Drona cave.Tapkeshwar Mahadev Temple is renowned for its natural cave environment and the unique occurrence of water droplets continuously falling onto the Shiva Lingam from the cave ceiling, giving the temple its name—Tapkeshwar, derived from the Hindi word "tapak," meaning to drip.The temple can be visited year-round, but the best time to visit is during the cooler months from October to March. Visiting during the monsoon season (July-August) also offers a unique experience with the river in full flow, but the area can be slippery. Learn more

FRI (Forest Research Institute)

Forest Research Institute Established in the year 1906, the Forest Research Institute is a premier institution in the field of forestry research in India. Nestled in the serene backdrop of the Doon Valley, Forest Research Institute is also a significant tourist attraction of Dehradun. The Institute has been used to film various Bollywood films.The best time to visit FRI is during the cooler months from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and suitable for exploring the extensive campus and its outdoor exhibits.The Forest Research Institute in Dehradun stands as a testament to India's rich forestry heritage and continues to play a crucial role in forestry education and research. Its stunning architecture and serene environment make it a must-visit for anyone interested in nature, history, or science. Learn more

Paltan Bazaar

Paltan Bazaar is located in the heart of Dehradun, spanning a 1.5km stretch between the Clock Tower and the railway station. From local spices, confectionery items & street food to goods like apparel, appliances and artefacts, this busy market has it all. Considered as Dehradun's main bazaar, it is the best place for shopping in Dehradun.

Shopping Experience

  • Variety of Goods: The market offers an extensive array of products, including clothing, footwear, jewelry, electronics, books, handicrafts, and household items. It's a one-stop destination for all kinds of shopping needs.
  • Local Specialties: Paltan Bazaar is a great place to buy local handicrafts, souvenirs, and traditional garments. You can find authentic Pahari shawls, woolen clothes, and other regional items.
  • Street Food: The market is also famous for its street food. Visitors can enjoy a variety of local delicacies such as samosas, chaat, momos, and sweets. There are numerous small eateries and food stalls offering delicious and affordable snacks.

Paltan Bazaar offers a quintessential Dehradun shopping experience with its lively atmosphere, diverse product range, and delightful street food. It’s a must-visit for anyone looking to soak in the local culture and enjoy a day of shopping and dining in the city.Learn more

Dehradun Zoo

Dehradun Zoo Located at the base of the Shivalik range, Dehradun Zoo, formerly known as Malsi Deer Park, is a zoological garden. It is home to two horned Deer, tigers, Neelgai, Peacock and many other animals. This well maintained zoological garden is an ideal place for a picnic.Dehradun Zoo, originally known as Malsi Deer Park, serves as a conservation center and a recreational area. It is situated at the base of the Shivalik Range and provides a natural habitat for a variety of wildlife species. The zoo is spread over a significant area and offers visitors a chance to observe animals in a setting that closely resembles their natural environment.The best time to visit Dehradun Zoo is during the cooler months from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and conducive for outdoor activities. Visiting during the early morning or late afternoon can also enhance the experience, as animals are often more active during these times. Learn more

Tapovan Temple

Tapovan Temple is located along the Dehradun-Rajpur Road. It is a picturesque valley, surrounded by lush greenery and calm meadows. Situated just above the Gangotri Glacier, the place also attracts trekking activities. The ashram also holds periodic yoga courses.Tapovan Ashram is known for its tranquil environment, making it an ideal place for meditation, yoga, and spiritual practices. The ashram provides a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, offering visitors a chance to rejuvenate their minds and bodies amidst nature.The best time to visit Tapovan Ashram is during the cooler months from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and conducive to outdoor activities like trekking and yoga.Tapovan Ashram offers a unique blend of natural beauty, spiritual practice, and physical activity, making it a perfect destination for those seeking peace, self-discovery, and a deeper connection with nature and spirituality. Clock Tower Dehradun

Clock Tower Dehradun also known as "ghanta ghar" is a very famous tourist attraction. It was a renowned landmark of the city and was built by the British before India got its independence. The clock does not tick at present, but it is said that years ago when it was built, the peak of it was such that a person on the other end of the city could hear it as well.The Clock Tower, located in the heart of Dehradun, is a prominent and historical structure that stands as a symbol of the city. It is a major point of reference for locals and a popular spot for visitors.The Clock Tower in Dehradun is more than just a timekeeping structure; it is a historical icon and a central part of the city’s cultural and social life. Its unique architecture, historical significance, and vibrant surroundings make it a must-visit landmark for anyone exploring Dehradun. Learn more

Maldevta

Maldevta located in Shripur, 18 km away from the ISBT, is a popular vacation spot in Dehradun. It offers one of the vistas of nature’s perfection, with the great Himalayas in the backdrop, the pristine Song River gurgling through thick shrubberies and little birds hopping and chirping about the area.Maldevta is renowned for its lush green surroundings, clear streams, and picturesque landscapes. It is situated on the outskirts of Dehradun and provides a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.Maldevta offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, tranquility, and recreational activities, making it a must-visit destination for anyone looking to experience the serene and picturesque landscapes of Dehradun. Whether you are a nature lover, an adventure enthusiast, or someone seeking a peaceful retreat, Maldevta has something to offer for everyone. Learn more
